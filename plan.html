Goal of the website is to increase number of customers playing volleyball at slammers by at least 50%.

Google ads, Facebook, and general search-engine searches

Success Evaluation:

Success of the website can be measured in multiple ways from number of responses to team sign-up form,
Google Analytics from search-engine and Google ads clicks, to measuring the number of people playing Volleyball and profits

Target Audience:

Majority of people who would click on this website would mainly be tech-savvy user either interested in playing
volleyball or looking to try a new sport or hobby.

They would be both male and female, either high-school aged kids (13 - 17yo) wanting to join a team and partake in an after-school activity,
they could also be people who have finished school recently and are looking to take up a sport to meet new people,
or they could be families looking for a weekend activity to do with kids.

These three groups of people would all be well used to the internet so the page would not have to be computer-illiterate friendly
as it would for the older users.
